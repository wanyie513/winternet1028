Profile

Who is he?

Dong Sicheng, known by his stage name Winwin, is a member of WayV, NCT, NCT U and NCT 127.

Born

28 October 1997

Winwin was born in Lucheng District, Wenzhou City, Zhejiang Province. He also has a sister living in New Zealand.

Teenage Life

2009

In 2009, he was admitted to the High School Attached to Beijing Dance Academy with first place in the art dance major. Although Winwin was selected by South Korea's SM Entertainment talent scout near the school when he was in high school, he went to South Korea for a month of training and returned to China to continue his studies because he wanted to focus on his studies.

University

2015

In 2015, Winwin was admitted to the Department of Performance of the Central Academy of Drama, and won first place in the national dance performance examination of Beijing Dance Academy, the 10th place in the performance major of the Central Academy of Drama, and the 7th place in the performance major of the Communication University of China.

Rookies life

2016

In the first year of his university life, Winwin decided to join SM Company as a trainee. He made his debut through SM Entertainment's pre-star group, which was named SM Rookies on Jan 5, 2016.

Debut

2016

On 3 July, 2016, he was announced as a member of NCT Seoul Branch, NCT 127. July 10, 2016: Made his debut on the sub-unit NCT 127 with the first mini-album, NCT #127.

NCT U (2018)

2018

February 18, 2018: Made his debut on the sub-unit NCT U with the first full album, NCT 2018 Empathy title song, "Boss".

NCT2018

2018

March 14, 2018: Participated on the project unit NCT 2018 with the first full album, NCT 2018 Empathy.

Rest

23 November 2018

On November 23, 2018, it was officially announced that Winwin would not participate in the new album of the group NCT 127 as he was preparing for his debut in China.

WayV

17 January 2019

On January 17, 2019, Made his debut on the sub-unit WayV with the digital EP, The Vision.

Resonance Pt.1

12 October 2020

Participated on the project unit NCT 2020 with the second full album repackage, NCT Resonance Pt. 1.

Resonance Pt.2

23 November 2020

Participated on the project unit NCT 2020 with the second full album repackage, NCT Resonance Pt. 2.

NCT 2020: Resonance

4 December 2020

Participated on the project unit NCT 2020 with the second full album final single, Resonance.

WayV third mini-album

10 March 2021

On 10 March, 2021, participated in the third mini-album by WayV, Kick Back.
