Music Video (NCT127)

NCT 127 is the second sub-unit of NCT. They made their debut as a seven member boy group on July 7, 2016 with the release of "Firetruck". The number 127 refers to the longitude for Seoul, where they are based. On 22 November, 2018, Winwin started hiatus from NCT 127 due to his participation in WayV.
